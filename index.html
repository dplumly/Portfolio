About me

I was asked as a kid, “What do you want to be when you grow up?” Fireman? Engineer? Nintendo Track and Field champion? I could never figure out what to do and dreamed of having an epiphany fall from the sky describing the path to take. Easy right? Well, things weren’t that easy, and if something falls from the sky, get out of the way!

Since I didn’t know where to start, but couldn’t sit in a bean bag forever, I began to think of what made me happy. The activities I did in my free time came to mind. Obviously, becoming a Nintendo Track and Field champion was out so, I went with my second choice; to build and create. Creating has been the driving force in my life, and luckily I found a career path to enable this. Now I have a job where I work all the time, but at the same time, I don’t work at all.
