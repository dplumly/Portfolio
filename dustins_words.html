Dustin's Words

Overview

Imagine a life where you could understand everything that is being said, but physically canâ€™t communicate back. That is a common occurrence for people who suffer from Autism. The project started with Matt Reamer making a device for his brother Dustin to be able to communicate with his Mom over text messages.

We started with Dustin, but are looking to help others. We are finding issues with the cost and functionality of similar devices on the market. Our goal is to come up with solutions for anyone that suffers from communication complications and make devices obtainable for them.
